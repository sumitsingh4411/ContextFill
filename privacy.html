Privacy Policy — ContextFill AI

Last updated: 20 June 2026

ContextFill AI ("the extension") is built to respect your privacy. This policy explains what data the extension handles and how.

What we store

The extension stores the information you add — pasted details, uploaded resume content and the resume file, and settings — locally in your browser using Chrome Storage and IndexedDB. This data stays on your device. Non-secret settings sync via your Chrome account only if you have Chrome Sync enabled.

We do not run any servers, and we do not collect, sell, or share your personal data.

AI providers (your choice)

To understand form fields, the extension sends the field labels and your relevant saved data to the AI provider you configure:

  • DeepSeek or OpenAI (ChatGPT) — text is sent to their API using your own API key, solely to compute field matches. Their use of that data is governed by their respective privacy policies.

You can change or remove your provider and API key at any time in Settings.

Sensitive data

The extension automatically detects sensitive fields — passwords, OTP, CVV, credit card, bank account, and UPI PIN — and never auto-fills them.

Permissions

  • storage — save your data and settings locally.
  • activeTab, scripting — read and fill form fields on the page you choose.
  • contextMenus — the "Save selection to AI Memory" right-click action.
  • sidePanel — the extension's user interface.
  • tabs, host_permissions — identify the active tab and operate on any site where you ask it to fill a form.

Data deletion

Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored data. You can also delete individual saved items at any time from the Saved Contexts screen.
